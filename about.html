ABOUT

Drawer. currently based in Cheltenham, UK

I am El, I draw in 003 fineline pen, in black or grey, with colour work done with drawing inks. Mostly individual pieces, but occasionally a triptych, series or ongoing project such as Pteridomania. It is a project of fern portraits begun in AoTeAroa, New Zealand in 2019 and continued back in the UK through 2022.
